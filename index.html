First... the basics

Understanding why these are a celebrity-favorite braiding style is key! “Knotless braids are single extension braids where the clients hair is braided first and [extension hair] is added to the braid,” Foster says. “It gives the appearance of a natural braid coming out of the scalp.” She notes that they generally lay flat to the scalp and are not big and bulky. Knotless braids are a variation of box braids.

Knotless box braids are endlessly popular for a good reason. The braided hairstyle can be styled in a variety of different ways, catering to your personal style.
