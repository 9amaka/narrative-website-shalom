5 step hair prep! lets begin..

STEP ONE

Preparing for knotless braids involves several steps to ensure a smooth and successful braiding process.

Ensure you have all the right equipment's with you as this will make the process swifter. The materials you will need are:

- The braiding hair

- A wide-tooth comb

- Hair clips or hairbands

- A hair moisturizer or a leave-in conditioner which will help to detangle and prep your hair

- Edge control or a styling gel which are both optional

STEP TWO

- Shampooing - Make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo (this is so that the natural oils and moisture your hair provides can be retained allowing your hair to be soft and shiny.) to thoroughly cleanse your scalp and hair removing all signs of build-up and any dirt.

- Conditioning - Next apply a moisturising conditioner to your hair and ensure to keep focus on the lengths and the ends of your hair as this is the oldest part. Gently detangle with your fingers and then your wide-tooth comb while the conditioner is still in and once complete rinse your hair thoroughly.

STEP THREE

Detangling - On damp hair begin detangling by first splitting your hair into sections of 4 or more if needed. Using your wide-tooth comb detangle comb through your hair starting at the tips and gradually working our way up.

Moisturizing - Apply a leave-in conditioner of your choice or a hair lotion/moisturizer to each section before this to avoid any breakage

STEP FOUR

Parting: use a rat-tail comb to create clean and even sections when braiding. Make sure to maintain the same size of each part so that they are not too large or too small.

STEP FIVE

Cutting and Preparing - The braiding hair should be cut to the desired length either in to 2,3 or even 4, however ensure that the length is still longer than your natural hair. Next you should separate the braiding hair to even smaller parts to get ready for braiding.
