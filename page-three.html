Code Snippet One Column

1.

Initial Braid - Begin braiding a small section of your natural hair without adding extensions. This creates the foundation for the knotless braids.

2.

To do (step six) apply product to the hair if needed then split the small section into three equal parts.

3.

Then once you have your three sections you are going to use your pincher fingers which are your index finger and your thumb to grab the peice of hair on the left hand side.

4.

Once you have completed this step you are then going to grab the peice of hair on ths right-hand side wih your other pincher fingers using the hand that is free this will then create the braid.

Do this step three times this will ensure that you have a solid base for your braid.

5.

Gradual Incorporation - After a few initial braids with your natural hair, start adding small amounts of extension hair to each section as you continue braiding.

6.

Blending Extensions - As you braid, ensure the extension hair blends seamlessly with your natural hair for a smooth and natural appearance. You can easily do this by tucking your natural hair behind in inside the extension as you braid down.

7.

And finally repeat this process all the way down until you have finished the braid.

TOP TIP!

Consistent Tension - Maintain consistent tension while braiding to avoid undue stress on your scalp. Keep the braids neat and uniform in size and tension.

8.

And lasty ensure that the ends of each braid is secured do this by braiding down until you can not anymore.
