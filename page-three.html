STEP SIX

Initial Braid - Begin braiding a small section of your natural hair without adding extensions. This creates the foundation for the knotless braids.

STEP SEVEN

Gradual Incorporation - After a few initial braids with your natural hair, start adding small amounts of extension hair to each section as you continue braiding.

Blending Extensions - As you braid, ensure the extension hair blends seamlessly with your natural hair for a smooth and natural appearance. You can easily do this by tucking your natural hair behind in inside the extension as you braid down.

TOP TIP!

Consistent Tension - Maintain consistent tension while braiding to avoid undue stress on your scalp. Keep the braids neat and uniform in size and tension.
