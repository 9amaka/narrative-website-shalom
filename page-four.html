This page will discuss the aftercare of braids and what to do once you have finished braiding the hair.

When it comes to aftercare for braids there are a few mustdo's

Follow these steps:

After you have finished with the braids you need to -

- Ensure that you have trimmed off excess hairs (fly-aways) sticking out anywhere and trim off any unnecessary length as needed.

- Boil hut water and pour into a jug or bowl. Dip the braids into the water for atleast 5 mins, if you are looking for curly ends you would need to have put the ends of the braids in rollers before dipping the hair.

- Then add a generous amount of hair mousse to the top over the head and flatten it on top and down the braids.

- Once you have covered most of the head tie a durag or a satin scarf onto the head and proceed to blowdry till dry

- Then remove the tie and spray the hair with sheen spray or hair spritz
